TEACHERS TRAINING

EFFECTIVE  TEACHING AND LEARNING

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

This teacher training program aims to enhance educators' skills and knowledge to create an engaging inclusive and effective learning environment.

PROGRAM OBJECTIVE

  • Improve teaching methodologies and strategies.
  • Enhance classroom management and organization skills
  • Develop effective assessment and feedback techniques
  • Foster a supportive and inclusive learning environment
  • Integrate technology into teaching and learning.

Program Outline

  • Module 1:
    • Effective Teaching Methodologies
  • Module 2:
    • Classroom Management and Organization
  • Module 3:
    • Assessment and Feedback
  • Module 4:
    • Inclusive Teaching Practice
  • Module 5:
    • Integrated Technology

PROGRAM DELIVERY

  1. Face-to-face training sessions
  2. Online modules and webinars
  3. Coaching and mentoring
  4. Peer support and collaboration

PROGRAM EVALUATION

  1. Pre-program and post-program surveys
  2. Observation of teaching practices
  3. Review of lesson plans and resources
  4. Feedback from students and peers

PROGRAM DURATION

  1. 5-day face-to-face training program
  2. 10-week online program with weekly webinars

TARGET AUDIENCE

  1. Primary and secondary school teachers
  2. School administrators and leaders
  3. Educators from diverse backgrounds and subjects
